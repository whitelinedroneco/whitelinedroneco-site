About WhiteLine Drone Co.

Founded on a passion for flight and an eye for detail, WhiteLine Drone Co. provides professional aerial photography and videography services that elevate your projects. Our team blends technical expertise with artistic vision to capture stunning imagery from perspectives you never thought possible.

We believe that every project is unique. Whether you’re showcasing a property, monitoring a build, or memorializing a special event, our mission is to deliver precision from above and purpose in every frame.

Coverage Area

Based in Murfreesboro, Tennessee, we proudly serve the Middle Tennessee region and beyond. From Nashville to the rolling hills of the surrounding counties, we’re ready to travel to capture your story.

Our Promise

Safety, creativity, and customer satisfaction are at the core of what we do. We are fully licensed and insured, and we fly in compliance with FAA regulations. Let us bring your vision to life with clarity and confidence.
